Welcome to sage
Thanks for testing out sage! sage is a meal planning application to encourage healthier eating and better financial choices. There are 3 main views: the recipes tab where you will add, edit and view your recipes, the meal plan tab where you can plan your meals for each day of the week, and the shopping list tab which generates a shopping list for you based on your meal plan.

For this prototype, there is no backend functionality, so DO NOT REFRESH THE PAGE or your data will be lost.
